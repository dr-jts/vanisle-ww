Southern Vancouver Island Whitewater

Puntledge River

Run Lower Puntledge Class III+
Level 65 cms - good
85 cms - prime 		Character medium volume, wide, many ledges & waves
Season when there is a dam release Boats kayaks
Gauge BC Hydro gauge Length 2 h
Put-in Below bridge over Stotan falls. Walk down road 50 m to steep trail. Can also walk 200m to a branch road under powerlines, then follow access road to river. Put in below "Gnarly Hole"
Takeout At the bridge N of town
Features
  • "Put-in Waves" - no info on this stretch
  • "Gnarly Hole" - avoid by staying L. Spot by seeing powerlines over river - hole is immediately below.
  • "Three Little Piggies" - 3 play holes on R against bank
  • "Splits #1" - stay R of island
  • Tarp-n-Play Hole - on R. Good eddy feeds hole. Can also eddy out in large calm eddy on R below and walk back up. Can access by trail as well.
  • "Splits #2" - stay R of island
  • Spectator Hole- on R close to bank. Spot by wooden railing at observation point on R bank
  • River-wide Hole - easiest line is on R (easy to avoid at moderate levels, but harder at high levels. Expect this after a R bend in the river, shortly below Spectator Hole
  • Weir - portage on the L. Spot this by numerous warning signs on both sides of river.
  • "Weir waves" - wave train against cliff on R
  • Powerhouse - on R.
  • Graham's Waves - against bank on L. Upper waves must be caught on the fly. To attain again carry boats up R bank.
Comments A popular play run with numerous waves and holes to catch. See Puntledge River - Jewel of Vancouver Island
